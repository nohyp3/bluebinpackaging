About this item

Air Bags, Air Pillows or Inflatable Air Cushions are a very common type of packaging included inside boxes. They are often included in amazon packages to fill up free space in the box or to provide cushioning for the item(s). This type of packaging is not recyclable in all municplaities due to it usually being made of a plastic film.

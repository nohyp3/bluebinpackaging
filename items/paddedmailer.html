Padded Mailer

About this item

Padded mailers are a very common type of packaging used for items that will need some protection while shipping. These mailers may be made of different materials, some seem to have a paper or polyethyelene outer while having a bubble padded inner. Some are entirely made of paper and some even claim to be made from compostable materials. The padded mailers made from paper and lined with a bubble padding are not recyclable as it is difficult to separate the recyclable material from the unrecyclable material. Mailers made with a plastic outer and a padded inner may be recyclable in your municipality. Mailers made entirely out of paper are reyclable. NOTE: not all "compostable" mailers will be compostable in your muncipality.

Envelope

About this item

This is the most common type of envelope, used for sending letters, bills, notices and other small items. These envelopes are easily recyclable. These envelopes may use wax paper to protect the adhesive before use. Wax paper cannot be recycled. These envelopes may have a litte window that is covered by a plastic film which allows for the recipient's address to be seen when it is printed on the letter (see image below).

Not all cities will recycle these envelopes with windows. However, you are able to remove the window and recycle the rest of the envelope easily.

About the site

ever been confused about where packaging goes?

I was frustrated with the lack of information available online. With the growth of e-commerce, there is only going to be a growing amount of packaging used to ship and protect products. I created this site to have easily accessible information about the different types of packaging and which bins they should be disposed in within the different major cities of Canada. This site is a personal project.
